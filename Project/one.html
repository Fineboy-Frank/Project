Favourite Foods

Nigerian Jollof Rice

This my favourite food by far and it's one of the best dishes in Africa. It is very tasty when prepared properly and you can easily prepare it with a low budget.

Eguisi and Garri

This is my go-to meal when I want something heavy and tasty. No matter where you come from, you will definitely love this meal once you've tried it.
